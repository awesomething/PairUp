Our Promise

Most times taking a part-time or self-paced course can be boring and lonely. Having an accountability partner or alumni to help, when you are stuck is a relieving experience. No more feeling awkward to ask questions on slack. Using a scheduling tool and matching algorithm makes it easier for both alumnis/experts who want to help and students who need help.

How does it work?

STEP 1 - Create a profile and select available days & time.
STEP 2 - Click to pick topics related to the course you are studying (E.g web development) or project you need help with.
STEP 3 - View other profiles and select similar time schedules to get matched for a video call to share screens.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for?

If you just joined a bootcamp or started an online part-time course and need some help often with your challenges, this is for you. Also, if you are an alumni and want to help with specific topics you are good at, accept to match with a beginner to help

How do you qualify people who join?

At the moment, being part of a bootcamp or an online school taking a part-time/self-paced course is required to join the platform. It is important so you don't slow any partner down or waste anybody's time.

What if I am not part of a bootcamp or taking online course?

You would be on our waiting list after you fill the form.

When should we expect the first version and what is the cost?

In March 2021, exact dates would be emailed to you and it is free.

Is getting a partner guaranteed?

It depends on your availability. The more days and times you pick, the better your chances are of getting matched with a partner. Plus you can both save your schedule on your calendar as a reminder.
